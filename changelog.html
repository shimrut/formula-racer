v 0.344 / March 2026

Jade Spiral Release

New Track: Jade Spiral Circuit. Features a long pit straight, a tightening spiral at the top, a wide sweep on the right, and a lower infield loop.

Gameplay & Feel:

  • / Lap times now use simulation time — frame drops and backgrounded tabs no longer inflate your time.
  • / Smoother motion — car position and rotation interpolated between physics steps, less jitter on lower-end devices.
  • / Countdown — all three red lights show before green / GO.
  • / Skid marks stay visible even when the game skips frames.
  • / Finish line & checkpoints register correctly when scraping or hugging walls.

Mobile & Input:

  • / Steering and buttons reset when the tab loses focus or you switch apps.
  • / Best lap time stays correct when switching tracks quickly.
  • / Share and input behavior improved on mobile.
