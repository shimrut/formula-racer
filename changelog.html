Changelog

Evolution of VectorGP. Updates, fixes, and circuit releases.

v 0.5 / April 2026

Global leaderboards

Per-track global boards are here. You choose whether a lap counts, then check the standings from your rank.

What changed:

  • /Ranked laps go worldwide. Set a track to RANKED for the global board, or LOCAL to keep times private. Time Trial and Session each have their own list.
  • /Open the board from Rank. Tap Rank on the track card or after a run to see the leaders and your place. Other players appear as anonymous names.
v 0.47 / March 2026

Track Visibility & UX Refresh

This update improves how the game introduces itself and how the wider site reads. Track availability, onboarding, support pages, and underlying stability all got a cleanup pass.

What changed:

  • /Visual updates now make it clearer that multiple tracks are available when you browse the selection flow.
  • /New-player onboarding and the How to Play modal were refined so the opening experience reads more clearly.
  • /The About and Changelog pages were updated for a cleaner presentation and better navigation.
  • /Various bug fixes and vulnerability hardening were applied across the experience.
v 0.46 / March 2026

Session Mode + Flow Update

One bigger update across the whole run flow. Session mode, result screens, onboarding, and controls have all been cleaned up in one pass.

What changed:

  • /Each track now lets you choose between Time Trial and Session before you start.
  • /Session mode now lets you keep running laps, pause anytime, and track your session best plus recent laps.
  • /Pause and results screens now use clearer action layouts, so it is faster to retry, change track, resume, finish, or share.
  • /The intro and track-select flow are cleaner, and steering input is more reliable across keyboard and on-screen controls.
v 0.45 / March 2026

Sakura Weave & Performance Update

New Track: Sakura Weave. Sakura Weave added to the main tracks.

Performance & Stability:

The game runs smoother and looks sharper. Overhauled the rendering and simulation loops to eliminate micro-stutters, especially during heavy cornering and drafting.

What changed:

  • / Skid marks and particle updates have been massively optimized to prevent frame drops during intense curves.
  • / The game canvas now better aligns with your specific device's pixel ratio, delivering a sharper look on high-resolution displays.
  • / Progression and session states are now properly maintained when switching between different tracks.
v 0.44 / March 2026

Track Select Polish

The track picker is easier to read. It now gives clearer feedback while you browse and keeps the presentation more consistent between screens.

What changed:

  • / The track picker now shows where you are in the lineup, so it is easier to see how many tracks are available.
  • / Track previews now sit more cleanly inside the selector and result screens.
  • / Personal bests shown on track cards now stay more reliable while moving between tracks and runs.
v 0.43 / March 2026

Flow & Presentation Update

The run loop is cleaner. Getting from finish or crash back into another lap now reads more clearly.

What changed:

  • / The restart button now reads Race Again.
  • / The launch sequence no longer flashes green lights under the GO! callout.
  • / The speed-line effect around the car has been removed for a cleaner race view.
v 0.42 / March 2026

Pre-Race Update

The pre-race flow has been reworked. Picking your next run is now cleaner, faster, and easier to read.

What changed:

  • / The track picker has been redesigned with a cleaner, more focused layout.
  • / Each option now shows your personal best directly in the card.
  • / You can now jump back into track selection from the HUD with a dedicated Change Track button.
  • / A new race-helmet menu gives faster access to How to Play, About, and Changelog.
  • / A new Support button has been added to the header.
v 0.41 / March 2026

Cedar Ridge + PB Update

New Track: Cedar Ridge Circuit. Cedar Ridge rotates into the main tracks slot, replacing Harbor Park Loop.

Personal Bests:

  • / Your 5 PBs moved to the HUD stats bar, so they are easier to open between runs.
  • / Opening PBs from a win or crash screen now returns cleanly to that same result modal when you back out.
  • / The HUD PB button stays disabled during live runs, then re-enables after a finish or crash.
v 0.4 / March 2026

Race Presentation Upgrade

The track visuals got a proper upgrade. Finish lines now look fully checkered, tire barriers read like actual tire stacks, and the car finally uses the correct yellow cockpit accent.

Racing Experience:

  • / Finish lines are now fully checkered instead of dashed, both on track and in shared lap previews.
  • / Tire walls look like actual tire stacks, with depth, sidewall shading, and tread detail.
  • / The car visuals were corrected so the yellow cockpit accent reads properly instead of blending into the tire color.

Performance & Stability:

  • / Core race updates were optimized so collision checks and run history handling stay lighter during play.
  • / Longer sessions stay cleaner and more stable, especially when traces, replays, or repeated runs would previously pile up extra work.
v 0.344 / March 2026

Jade Spiral Release

New Track: Jade Spiral Circuit. Features a long pit straight, a tightening spiral at the top, a wide sweep on the right, and a lower infield loop.

Gameplay & Feel:

  • / Lap times now use simulation time — frame drops and backgrounded tabs no longer inflate your time.
  • / Smoother motion — car position and rotation interpolated between physics steps, less jitter on lower-end devices.
  • / Countdown — all three red lights show before green / GO.
  • / Skid marks stay visible even when the game skips frames.
  • / Finish line & checkpoints register correctly when scraping or hugging walls.

Mobile & Input:

  • / Steering and buttons reset when the tab loses focus or you switch apps.
  • / Best lap time stays correct when switching tracks quickly.
  • / Share and input behavior improved on mobile.
