How to Play

Free arcade racing game in your browser. Pick a mode, learn the line, and chase your best result on each track or in today’s Daily GP. Keep watch for a new track each Wednesday at 07:00 UTC.

Crash course

  1. Choose a mode, then a track to compete
  2. Start the race and steer with the arrows
  3. Tiny steering inputs beat big corrections
  4. Finish clean laps to set a personal best

Game Modes

Daily GP
One featured event per day with its own track, modifiers, best result, and leaderboard. Challenges can be single-lap, multi-lap total-time, or crash-budget runs.
Time Trial
Single-lap runs. Crash or finish - your 5 best personal records are saved. Pure arcade racing at its core.
Session
Multi-lap practice. Crashes reset you to the start line instantly without breaking your flow. Chase consistency lap after lap.
